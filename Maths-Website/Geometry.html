Lesson Note — Area and Perimeter of Shapes

Lesson Objectives

  • Define perimeter and area.

  • Calculate perimeter and area of rectangle, square, triangle, parallelogram, trapezium, rhombus, and kite.

  • Solve real-life problems involving area and perimeter.

Perimeter is the distance around a particular shape.

Formulas

ShapePerimeterArea
Rectangle2(l + w)l × w
Square4 × sideside²
Trianglea + b + c½ × base × height
Parallelogram2(a + b)base × height
Trapeziumsum of all sides½ × (sum of parallel sides) × height
Rhombus4 × side½ × (diagonal1 × diagonal2)
Kite2 × (sum of adjacent sides)½ × (diagonal1 × diagonal2)

Worked Examples

Example 1: Find the perimeter of a rectangular football field 100m long and 60m wide.

Perimeter = 2 x ( L + B)

perimeter = 2 x (100 + 60)

= 2 x 160

= 320m

Answer: 320m

Example 2: Find the area of a trapezium whose parallel sides are 12cm and 10cm and the height is 8cm.

Area = 1/2 x (a + b) x h

Area = 1/2 x (12 + 10) x 8

= 1/2 x 22 x 8

= 11 x 8

=88cm2

Answer: 88cm2

Example 3: If the perimeter of a square is 64cm. Find its area.

Perimeter = 4 x L

L = Perimeter ÷ 4

Length = 64 ÷ 4

= 16cm

The area of a square = L2

= 16 x 16

= 256cm2

Answer: 256cm2

Example 4: Find the area of a triangle whose base and height are 24cm and 6cm respectively.

Area = 1/2 x base x height

Area = 1/2 x 24 x 6

= 12 x 6

= 72cm2

Answer: 72cm2

Example 5: Calculate the perimeter of a parallelogram whose parallel sides are 200m and 60m respectively.

Perimeter = 2 x (a + b)

Perimeter = 2 x (200 + 60)

= 2 x 260

= 520m

Answer: 520m

Example 6: The perimeter of a rectangle is 100cm. If the breadth is 20cm, find the area of the rectangle.

Perimeter = 2 x (L + B)

L + B = 100 ÷ 2

L + B = 50

L = 50 - 20

L = 30cm

Area of a rectangle = L x B

= 30 x 20

= 600cm2

Answer: 600cm2

Example 7: The area of a rectangle is 225m2. If the length is 25m, what is the perimeter?

Area = L x B

Breadth = Area ÷ Length

Breadth = 225 ÷ 25

Breadth = 9m

Perimeter = 2 x (L + B)

= 2 x (25 + 9)

= 2 x 34

= 68m

Answer: 68m

Assesment on — Area and Perimeter of Shapes

Section A: Calculate Perimeter

  1. Rectangle: l = 12 cm, w = 8 cm

  2. Square: side = 10 cm

  3. Triangle: sides = 7 cm, 8 cm, 9 cm

  4. Parallelogram: sides = 10 cm and 6 cm

  5. Trapezium: sides = 5 cm, 7 cm, 4 cm, 6 cm

  6. Rhombus: side = 9 cm

  7. Kite: adjacent sides = 6 cm, 8 cm

Section B: Calculate Area

  1. Rectangle: l = 15 cm, w = 10 cm

  2. Square: side = 12 cm

  3. Triangle: base = 8 cm, height = 5 cm

  4. Parallelogram: base = 10 cm, height = 6 cm

  5. Trapezium: parallel sides = 8 cm, 6 cm, height = 5 cm

  6. Rhombus: diagonals = 10 cm, 8 cm

  7. Kite: diagonals = 12 cm, 9 cm

Section C: Word Problems

  1. A rectangular field is 50 m long and 30 m wide. Find perimeter and area.

  2. A square playground has side 25 m. Find perimeter and area.

  3. A triangular park has sides 20 m, 15 m, 25 m and height 12 m. Find perimeter and area.

  4. A parallelogram garden has base 18 m and height 10 m, sides 18 m and 12 m. Find perimeter and area.

  5. A rhombus-shaped kite has diagonals 14 m and 10 m. Find perimeter and area.

  6. A trapezium-shaped swimming pool has parallel sides 20 m and 12 m, other sides 8 m and 10 m, height 7 m. Find perimeter and area.

  7. A kite-shaped decoration has diagonals 15 m and 8 m, sides 10 m and 12 m. Find perimeter and area.
