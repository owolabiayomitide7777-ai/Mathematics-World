    Mathematical Formulas

    Below are some commonly used Formulas in Mathematics:

    1. Area of a Trapezium:

        A = 1/2 × (Sum of the parallel sides) × height
    2. Area of a Rectangle:

        A = length x breadth
    3. Perimeter of a Rectangle:

        P = 2 x (length + breadth)
    4. Area of a Triangle:

        A = 1/2 x base x height
    5. Area of a Square:

        A = (Length)2
    6. Perimeter of a Square:

        P = 4 x length
    7. Circumference or Perimeter of a Circle:

        P = 2 × π × r or π x d
    8. Area of a Circle:

        A = π × r2
    9. Volume of a Cube:

          V = L3
    10. Volume of a Cuboid:

        V = L x B x H
    11. Simple Interest:

        I = (P x R x T) /100
    12. The Principal of Interest:

        P = (100 x I)/(R x T)
    13. The Rate of Interest:

        R = (100 x I)/(P x T)
    14. The Time of Interest:

        T = (100 X I)/(P x R)
    15. Pythagoras Theorem:

        (Hypotenus)2 = (Opposite)2 + (Adjacent)2
    16. Area of a Parallelogram:

        A = base x height
    17. Perimeter of a Parallelogram:

        P = 2 x (a + b)
    18. Quadratic Formula:

        X = (-b ± √(b2 - 4 × a × c))/(2 × a)
    19. Area of a Rhombus:

        A = 1/2 x diagonal 1 x diagonal 2
    20. Average or Mean:

        A = (a + b + c + d + e)/5
    21. Profit or Gain:

        Profit = Selling Price - Cost Price
    22. Loss:

        Loss = Cost Price - Selling Price
    23. Profit Percentage:

        Profit% = Profit/(Cost price) × 100
    24. Loss Percentage:

        Loss% = Loss/(Cost price) × 100
    25. Error:

        Error = Actual Value - Wrong Value
    26. Percentage Error:

        Error% = Error/(Actual value) × 100
    27. Discount Percentage:

        Discount% = Discount/(Actual Price) x 100
    28. Commission Percentage:

        Commission% = Commission/(Actual Price) x 100
    29. Speed:

        Speed = Distance/Time
    30. Distance:

        Distance = Speed x Time
    31. Time:

        Time = Distance/Speed
    32. Radius of a Circle:

        Radius = Diameter/2
    33. Diameter of a Circle:

        Diameter = 2 x Radius
    34. Curved Surface Area of a Cylinder:

        Surface Area = 2 x π × r x h
    35. Volume of a Cylinder:

        V = π × r2 x h
    36. Area of the base of a Cone:

        Area = π × r2
    37. Curved Surface Area of a Cone:

        Curved Surface Area = π × r x l
    38. Volume of a Cone:

        V = 1/3 x π × r2 x h
    39. Volume of a Pyramid:

        V = 1/3 x base area x height
    40. Volume of a Prism:

        V = area of cross-section x length
    41. Surface Area of Sphere:

        Surface Area = 4 x π × r2
    42. Volume of Sphere:

        V = 4/3 x π × r3
    43. Curved Surface Area of Hemisphere:

        Surface Area = 2 x π × r2
    44. Total Surface Area of Hemisphere:

        Total Surface Area = 3 x π × r2
    45. Volume of a Hemisphere:

        V = 2/3 x π × r3
    46. Complementary Angle:

        a0 + b0 = 900
    47. Supplementary Angle:

        a0 + b0 = 1800
    48. MidPoints:

        M = ((X1 + X2)/2,(Y1 + Y2)/2)
    49. Distributive Property:

        a x (b + c) = (a x b) + (a x c)
    50. Commutative Property:

        a + b = b + a

        a x b = b x a
    51: Square of a Binomial

        (a + b)2 = a2 + 2ab + b

        (a - b)2 = a2 - 2ab + b
    52: Subtraction of two square:

        (a2 - b2) = (a + b)(a - b)
    53. Sum of Cubes:

        x3 + y3 = (x + y) (x2 - xy + y2)

        x3 - y3 = (x - y) (x2 + xy + y2)
    54. Fundamental Identity:

        (Sinθ)2 + (Cosθ)2 = 1
    55. Double Angle Identities:

        (Sin2θ) = 2SinθCosθ

        (Cos2θ)= (Cosθ)2 - (Sinθ)2
